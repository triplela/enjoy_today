# Design Context

## Users
- **Who**: Anyone looking for activity inspiration — primarily casual users who are bored or have free time
- **Context**: Deciding what to do on a day off, weekend, or spontaneous free time
- **Job to be done**: Reduce decision fatigue by providing curated, context-aware activity suggestions based on simple inputs (how many people, indoor/outdoor)

## Brand Personality
**Playful, friendly, refined**

- **Playful**: Approachable and fun, but not childish
- **Friendly**: Inviting and warm, like a helpful companion
- **Refined**: Clean and intentional, every detail considered

**Emotional goal**: Light & enjoyable — make choosing an activity feel effortless and delightful

## Aesthetic Direction

**Visual tone**: Minimalist monochrome with playful touches. Clean whitespace, strong typography, intentional color pops. Think Apple.com meets friendly personality.

**Theme**: Light monochrome base with strategic accent colors. Clean, geometric, spacious.

**Must avoid**:
- Excessive decoration or visual noise
- Dark moody aesthetics
- Corporate/formal stiffness
- Cluttered layouts
- Warm earthy palettes (previous direction)

**References**:
- Apple.com: Clean product pages, strong typography, generous whitespace
- Linear: Minimal dark interfaces with refined details
- Notion: Clean workspace that feels friendly

## Design Principles

1. **Radical simplicity** — Every element earns its place; remove everything non-essential
2. **Typography as hero** — Strong type hierarchy, beautiful geometric fonts
3. **Whitespace is content** — Generous breathing room creates calm focus
4. **Subtle playfulness** — Friendly without being cartoonish; refined whimsy
5. **Monochrome foundation** — Black, white, grays with strategic color accents
